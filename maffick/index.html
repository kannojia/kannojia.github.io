3. We just give the name of hero or actress and they are supposed to find out his/her first movie or vice versa (for eg: Om Shanti Om for Deepika) .
4. We show them a picture ( a famous clip) of a movie then they guess that movie and cut its name from the ticket.
5. Logo of famous Hollywood stuff (for Eg shield of Captain America, logo of Batman, Superman)
6. Some clips of famous Hollywood series and movies are also included like (Sherlock, Iron Man, FRIENDS, Harry Potter, Dark Knight, Avengers, Despicable me etc.)

Event Rules: As there are 90 symbols in Game and each ticket has 15 symbols. So, 6 tickets have 15*6 90 symbols. In all these 6 tickets, each symbol from 1-90 occurs in one ticket only. Therefore, when a CUE is called, it will be present in one and only one ticket.

1. There are 3 rows and 9 columns in every Tambola Ticket.
2. Every ticket has exactly 15 symbols.
3. There will be at least 1 symbol in every column.
4. A Ticket cannot have same symbol more than once.
5. The players selection will be based on first come first basis.

HOLLY BOLLY TAMBOLA

Tambola Tickets As a rule, each player must buy at least one ticket to enter a game. A typical 90 ball Tambola ticket consists of 3 rows and 9 columns which make 27 spaces. Each row has a total of 5 numbers printed on it(in our case some clues of movies). A column can have 1, 2 or the maximum3 clues printed on it.

Gameplay The objective of the game is to mark/ dab all the names/clues found in the ticket as called by the dealer/host. The player who first mark all the names /clues in a winning pattern and calls a win is declared as the WINNER of that pattern after the dealer/host checks his ticket and verify it with names drawn.

If your claimed winning pattern is wrong, it will be called BOOGY and you cannot continue the game i.e. You are disqualified.

The game ends when all 90 clues are drawn, or when a winner is declared for all the patterns of the game, whichever comes first.

Tambola Winning Patterns In order to win in Tambola or Bingo, you need to match winning combinations. Some of the popular winning combinations in a Tambola game are given below:

Top Line: The ticket with all the names of the top row dabbed fastest.
Middle Line: The ticket with all the names of the middle row dabbed fastest.
Bottom Line: The ticket with the names of the bottom row dabbed fasted.
Full House: The ticket with all the 15 numbers marked first.
